Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Progressive muscle relaxation is an exercise that teaches you how to relax your muscles.

Progressive muscle relaxation is a mediation exercise that teaches you how to relax your muscles using a two-step process. First, you tense particular muscle groups in your body/ Then, you release the tension and notice how your muscles feel.
